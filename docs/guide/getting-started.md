# Getting started

Welcome to Fateh HR. This guide gets you from "just installed the app" to "checked in and ready to work" in under three minutes.

## What Fateh HR does

Fateh HR is the mobile app for your HR life. You use it to:

- **Check in / check out** of your workday with location + optional selfie
- **Apply for leave** and see your balance
- **File expense claims** with receipt photos
- **View and download payslips**
- **See your attendance calendar** for any month

It works on any Android phone, and also runs as a web app in your browser. Most importantly — **it works offline**. If you're in a warehouse with no signal or out on a delivery route, just use the app as normal. Your check-ins and requests queue up locally and sync the moment you're back online.

## Before your first login

You'll need:

- Your **work email** (the one your HR team registered for you)
- Your **password** (sent to you by HR — reset from the Desk if you've forgotten it)
- The Fateh HR **app installed** — see [Install the app](/guide/install)

::: tip
If you don't know your email or password yet, ask your HR team. They can create or reset your account from the Frappe HR desk.
:::

## Your first 3 minutes

Once the app is installed, here's the quickest path to a working setup:

1. **Open Fateh HR**
2. **Log in** with your work email and password → [details](/guide/login)
3. **Set a PIN** (4–6 digits) — this is what you'll use every day instead of the full password → [details](/guide/login#set-your-pin)
4. **Enable biometric unlock** when asked — skip typing the PIN by using your fingerprint / face → [details](/guide/biometric)
5. You're on the home screen. Tap **"Check In"** to start your day.

From now on, you only need to enter your password again if you log out. Day to day it's PIN or fingerprint.

## What each tab does

| Tab | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Current status (IN / OUT), the check-in button, your last 3 check-ins, latest announcement |
| **Attendance** | Monthly calendar view — tap any day to see IN / OUT pairs and total hours |
| **Leave** | Your leave balances, request form, recent applications, and history |
| **Expense** | Totals (Claimed, Pending, Approved, Paid), new claim button, recent claims |
| **More** | Profile, payslips, announcements, notifications, sync errors, language, logout |

## When the sync bar turns orange

At the top of most screens you'll see a thin status bar:

- **Synced now** (grey) — everything saved to the server
- **5 changes pending · tap to sync** (amber) — you have offline items waiting
- **Syncing…** (blue) — actively uploading
- **Offline — working locally** (grey) — no network, but everything you do still saves locally
- **2 sync errors** (red) — something failed; tap to see why

If you see the red state, open it and tap **Retry**. It's almost always a temporary network hiccup.
