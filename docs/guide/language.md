# Language

Switch the whole app between **English** and **العربية** (Arabic) instantly.

## Changing language

1. Tap the **More** tab
2. Scroll to the **Language** card
3. Tap **English** or **العربية**

The app switches immediately — no restart needed.

## What changes

- All labels, buttons, and messages
- Form field names
- Date and time display (month abbreviations etc.)
- Text direction:
  - **English** — left to right (LTR)
  - **العربية** — right to left (RTL). The entire layout mirrors: back arrows, chevrons, sidebars, margins.

## What doesn't change

- Your data (names, addresses, descriptions) — those stay in whatever language they were entered in
- Company name / branding
- System-generated IDs (employee code, payslip name)

## Per-device setting

The language choice is saved on your device. Each phone / browser remembers its own preference. Logging out doesn't reset it.
