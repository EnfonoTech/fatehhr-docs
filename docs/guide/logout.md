# Log out

When you need to fully sign out — for example, if you're handing the phone to someone else, or you need to log in as a different user.

## How

1. Tap the **More** tab
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap the red **Log out** button

You're immediately taken back to the login screen.

## What log-out does

- Clears your session token
- Clears your cached PIN hash
- Clears the last-login-email shortcut (so the next user isn't greeted by your email)
- Clears the biometric enrolment flag
- **Keeps any pending offline items** (check-ins, leave, expense) — if the next user is you again and you log back in, they'll still be there waiting to sync

## What log-out does NOT do

- It doesn't delete your data on the server
- It doesn't clear your check-in history, leave applications, or payslips (those live on the server and reappear on next login)
- It doesn't uninstall the app
- It doesn't clear files already saved to your Documents folder (e.g. downloaded payslip PDFs)

## Before logging out: sync first

If you have **pending offline items** in the queue, they will still be there after logout — but they can only sync while you're logged in. Best practice:

1. Make sure the sync bar says **"Synced now"** before logging out
2. If it says **"3 changes pending"**, tap it and wait for sync to complete
3. Then log out

This avoids situations where someone else logs in, syncs their data, and your queued items stay stuck.

## Session timeout vs log-out

These are different:

- **Session timeout** (~2 hours of inactivity) — the app asks for your PIN / biometric again, but you stay logged in. Fast to unlock.
- **Log out** — full sign-out; next time you need your password.
